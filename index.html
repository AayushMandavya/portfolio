About Me

Greetings, I’m Aayush Mandavya. I’m a traveler and an explorer who likes to discover new parts of the world and take on new challenges. I have a passion for computer science and I’m currently studying it at the bachelor level. I also have a talent for Photoshop and videography, which I practice in my free time. I enjoy creating and editing videos of my travels and experiences. I’m currently working as an intern at Alternative Technology, where I learn new skills and contribute to innovative projects. I’m a friendly and open-minded person who values learning and growth.
